LION

African lions are the most social of all big cats and live together in groups or “prides.” A pride consists of about 15 lions. A lion can run for short distances at 50 mph and leap as far as 36 feet.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average length: 1.7 to 2.5 meters
  • Average lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Grassland, Savanna, Dense Scrub and Open Woodland

Lions go on the hunt for food mostly from dusk till dawn. Female lions do 85-90% of the prides hunting, whilst the male lions patrol the territory and protect the pride. A female lion needs 5kg of meat a day. A male needs 7kg or more a day. Often known as the ‘king of the jungle’, most lions actually live in the savannah or grasslands. Just one population of wild forest-dwelling lions remains, in Gir Forest National Park, India.
